Graduate Student Homotopy Theory Seminar

Spring 2022

The seminar is held in 341 Altgeld Hall at 3pm each Friday. In the event that the seminar is moved online, the talk will be given in this Zoom room. The speakers and titles can be found below.

  • Organizational Meeting (January 28th)
  • Brian Shin (February 11th): An Introduction to Exodromy.
  • Timmy Feng (February 18th): Formalism of six operations and derived algebraic stacks (notes).
  • Zach Halladay (February 25th): Introduction to Equivariant Homotopy Theory and RO(G)-graded Cohomology.
  • No speaker planned (March 4th)
  • Likun Xie (March 11th): Applications of Dualizing Complex in Commutative Algebra.
    • Spring Break
  • Yigal Kamel (March 25th): An introduction to 2-categories (notes).
  • Liz Tatum (April 1st): Splitting $BP<2> ⋀ BP<2>$ at primes $p \ge 5$.
  • Samuel Hsu (April 8th): Towards a universal property of the ∞-equipment of enriched (∞,1)-categories.
  • Doron Grossman-Naples (April 15th): You Already Care About ∞-Topoi.
  • Langwen Hui (April 22nd): Rational Homotopy Theory.
  • Johnson Tan (April 29th)

Higher Category Theory Reading Group

Spring 2022

This reading group meets in 347 Altgeld Hall at 2pm each Friday. In the event that the reading group is moved online, a Zoom link will be sent to participants. It is planned to cover David Gepner's survey An Introduction to Higher Categorical Algebra and (some portion of) the first chapter of Jacob Lurie's Higher Algebra.

Fall 2021

This reading group met in 441 Altgeld Hall at 2pm each Friday. It covered the last two chapters of Markus Land's Introduction to Infinity-Categories.
